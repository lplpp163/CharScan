段落 / 時間English (spoken)中文對照
1. 問題0:00–0:40 Every carbon-removal project hits the same wall: measurement. How much carbon does a plant actually hold? Today, the only way to know is to cut it down and oven-dry it for days. It kills the plant, and it's far too slow and costly to repeat. So even projects that truly capture carbon often can't prove how much — and without proof, there's no credit. 畫面：簡報 Slide 2（Problem）。標題卡 + 「harvest → oven-dry 2–3 days → destroyed」的破壞性流程示意。 每個碳移除專案都會撞上同一道牆：量測。一株植物到底固了多少碳？今天唯一的辦法，是把它砍下來、烘乾好幾天。這會毀掉植株，又慢又貴、無法大量重複。於是就算真的固了碳，也常常證明不了多少——沒有證據，就沒有碳權。
2. 構想0:40–1:10 LeafCarbon replaces that with a single photo. You set up a simple stand, take one picture of a plant, and the model estimates its dry weight — and from that, its carbon — each with a confidence range. Non-destructive, and a few seconds instead of a few days. 畫面：簡報 Slide 3（The idea）。固定拍攝架（側拍）→ 一張照片 → 模型輸出（乾重 → 碳）。 LeafCarbon 把它換成一張照片。架個簡單的支架、拍一株植物，模型就估出它的乾重、再換算成碳量，每個都附一個信賴範圍。不破壞植株，幾秒就好，不用好幾天。
3. 實機 Demo1:10–2:40 Here's the tool. I pick one photo of a single lettuce. First, the model finds the green leaves and draws a box around them. Then — this is the heart of it — it reads a few simple measurements straight off that canopy: how tall the plant is, its shape, how dense the leaves are, how much green there is. It adds the weather the plant has lived through — sunlight, warmth, UV. Every one of these numbers shows up on screen; nothing is hidden in a black box. From them, the model predicts the dry weight, with a range showing how sure it is. Now watch the same plant grow — day 7, 20, 27, 35: the box gets bigger, the numbers rise, and the estimate climbs right along with the real plant. 畫面：切到互動 Demo（demo/index.html）實機螢幕錄影。依序點 Day 7 → 20 → 27 → 35：① 輸入照片 → ② 偵測冠層（綠色填色＋外框 bbox）→ ③ 模型輸入特徵面板（株高 / 寬高比 / 密實度 / 綠覆蓋 ＋ 溫度 / 日照 / GDD / UV，數值即時帶出）→ ④ 乾重＋信賴範圍 → ⑤ 碳量（× 0.47）。讓外框與特徵數值隨日數變大、估值上升。 這就是工具。我選一株萵苣的一張照片。模型先找出綠葉、框出範圍。接著——這是核心——它直接從冠層讀出幾個簡單的量：植株多高、外形如何、葉子多密、綠的多寡，再加上這株植物經歷過的天氣——日照、溫度、紫外線。這些數字全都顯示在畫面上，沒有藏在黑盒裡。模型據此預測乾重，並給出一個範圍表示有多確定。看同一株長大——第 7、20、27、35 天：框愈來愈大、數字往上跳，估值也跟著真實植株一起爬升。
4. 碳的換算2:40–3:15 Carbon comes straight from biomass. Dry plant matter is almost a fixed fraction carbon — about forty-seven percent, and across four thousand species it barely moves. So carbon is simply dry weight times zero-point-four-seven. We only have to get the weight right — no chemistry on each plant. We chose lettuce on purpose: a thirty-five-day cycle lets us test the method fast. 畫面：簡報 Slide 5（Carbon = biomass × 0.47）。等式大字 + IPCC 2006 / Ma et al. 2018 來源小字 + 「lettuce = fast validation crop, 35-day」標註。 碳量直接由生物量換算。乾掉的植物幾乎是固定比例的碳——約 47%，四千多個物種裡幾乎不變。所以碳就是乾重 × 0.47。我們只要把重量量準，不必對每株做化學分析。我們刻意挑萵苣：35 天的週期，讓我們能很快驗證方法。
5. 成績與成本3:15–4:05 How well does it work? On our own dataset — seventy-two lettuce plants over twenty-four days — the estimate lands close to the real oven-dried weight, an R-squared around zero-point-six-four. And we tested it the honest way: the model is always scored on a day it has never seen. This is a measured result, not a simulation. The win is the contrast — days and a dead plant become one photo and a few seconds, and you can measure the same plant again tomorrow. 畫面：簡報 Slide 6（Results）。三個大數字 R²≈0.64 / RMSE≈0.43 g / 留一日（72 株·24 天）；右側「days + destroyed」對「seconds + repeatable」對比；可疊一段 Demo 的信賴範圍特寫。 效果如何？在我們自己的資料集上——72 株萵苣、橫跨 24 天——估計值很接近真實的烘乾重量，R² 約 0.64。而且我們用誠實的方式測：模型每次都被拿沒看過的一天來打分。這是實測，不是模擬。最大的價值在對比——好幾天加一株死掉的植物，變成一張照片、幾秒鐘，而且同一株明天還能再量。
6. 邊界與收尾4:05–4:35 One honest boundary: this is a measurement, not a carbon credit — and lettuce is just our test crop, not the carbon sink. What we've built is a cheap, non-destructive ruler. The same ruler can move to the pools that really matter — forests, soils, restoration land — where cheap measurement is exactly what's missing today. 畫面：簡報 Slide 7（Scope）→ Slide 8（Close）。先打出「measurement ≠ credit · lettuce ≠ sink」誠實邊界，再切森林／土壤／復育場景，LeafCarbon 標誌定格。 一個誠實的邊界：這是量測，不是碳權；萵苣只是測試作物，不是碳匯標的。我們做的是一把便宜、非破壞的尺。這把尺可以移到真正重要的碳庫——森林、土壤、復育地——而那裡今天最缺的，正是便宜的量測。
