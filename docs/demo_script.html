段落 / 時間English (spoken)中文對照
1. 問題0:00–0:40 For any project that removes carbon, the hard part is usually the measurement — knowing how much carbon a plant actually holds. The standard way is to harvest the plant and oven-dry it for two to three days. It destroys the plant, and it is far too slow and costly to repeat at scale. So projects that genuinely capture carbon often cannot prove how much — and without proof, they cannot earn credit for it. 畫面：簡報 Slide 2（Problem）。標題卡 + 「harvest → oven-dry 2–3 days → destroyed」的破壞性流程示意。 對任何碳移除專案來說，難的通常是量測：一株植物到底固了多少碳。傳統做法要把植株採收、烘乾兩三天，會毀掉植株，也慢到、貴到無法大量重複。於是即使真的固了碳，也常常證明不了固了多少；沒有證據，就拿不到碳權。
2. 構想0:40–1:15 LeafCarbon turns that measurement into a single photo. You photograph one plant with a simple fixed setup, and a model estimates its dry biomass — and from that, its carbon — each with a confidence range. A small set of oven-dried samples calibrates the model just once. After that, every new plant is only a photo: non-destructive, and a few seconds instead of a few days. 畫面：簡報 Slide 3（The idea）。固定拍攝架（側拍）→ 一張照片 →「calibrate once」示意。 LeafCarbon 把這個量測變成一張照片。用簡單的固定裝置拍一株植物，模型估出它的乾生物量、再換成碳量，每個都附帶一個信賴範圍。少量烘乾樣本只需校正模型一次，之後每一株新植物就只是一張照片：非破壞，而且是幾秒、不是幾天。
3. 實機 Demo1:15–2:35 Here is the tool itself. I pick one photo of a single lettuce plant. The model first detects the green canopy — a simple ExG-plus-HSV segmentation — and crops it. From that canopy it reads side-view structure: how much green, the plant's height and shape. It combines this with the cumulative environment the plant has seen — light, growing-degree-days, UV. A Random Forest then turns these into a per-plant dry-weight estimate, with a ninety-five-percent confidence interval. Watch the same plant across its cycle — day seven, eighteen, twenty-seven, thirty-five — the estimate climbs as the plant actually grows. Because the model is interpretable, we can see which features carry the signal. 畫面：切到互動 Demo（demo/index.html）實機螢幕錄影。依序點 Day 7 → 18 → 27 → 35：① 輸入照片 → ② 偵測冠層（ExG+HSV，canopy %）→ ③ 乾重 + 95% CI 區間條 → ④ 碳量（× 0.47，mg C / plant）。讓估值隨日數上升。 這是工具本身。我選一株萵苣的一張照片。模型先偵測綠色冠層——用簡單的 ExG+HSV 分割——並裁切出來。再從冠層讀出側拍結構：綠的多寡、株高與形狀。接著結合這株植物累積經歷的環境——光照、生長度日、紫外線。隨機森林把這些轉成每株乾重估計，附 95% 信賴區間。看同一株走過整個週期——第 7、18、27、35 天——估值會隨植物真實長大而上升。而且模型可解釋，看得出是哪些特徵帶有訊號。
4. 碳的換算2:35–3:15 Carbon follows directly from biomass. The carbon fraction of dry plant matter is nearly constant — about forty-seven percent; across more than four thousand species it sits between forty-five and forty-eight. So carbon equals dry biomass times zero-point-four-seven. We only have to measure biomass well — no elemental analysis per plant. Lettuce is a fast validation crop we chose on purpose: a thirty-five-day cycle lets us tune and check the method quickly. 畫面：簡報 Slide 5（Carbon = biomass × 0.47）。等式大字 + IPCC 2006 / Ma et al. 2018 來源小字 + 「lettuce = fast validation crop, 35-day」標註。 碳量直接由生物量換算。植物乾物質的碳分率接近常數——約 47%；在四千多個物種上落在 45 到 48 之間。所以碳 = 乾生物量 × 0.47。我們只要把生物量量好，不需要對每株做元素分析。萵苣是我們刻意挑的快速驗證作物：35 天的週期讓我們能很快把方法調準、驗證。
5. 成績與成本3:15–4:15 On our own lettuce dataset — seventy-two plants across twenty-four days — the model predicts per-plant dry weight with an R-squared of about zero-point-six-four and an RMSE of about zero-point-four-three grams, under leave-one-day-out cross-validation. This is a measured result, not a simulation. The contrast with the standard method is the point: days and a destroyed plant become one photo and a few seconds, and the same plant can be measured again later. Every estimate carries its own uncertainty range, so we always know how confident it is. 畫面：簡報 Slide 6（Results）。三個大數字 R²≈0.64 / RMSE≈0.43 g / leave-one-day-out（72 株·24 天）；右側「days + destroyed」對「seconds + repeatable」對比；可疊一段 Demo 的 CI 區間條特寫。 在我們自己的萵苣資料集上——72 株、橫跨 24 天——模型估每株乾重的 R² 約 0.64、RMSE 約 0.43 克，採留一日交叉驗證。這是實測結果，不是模擬。和傳統方法的對比正是重點：數天又毀植株，變成一張照片、幾秒，而且同一株之後還能再量。每筆估計都帶自己的不確定度範圍，所以我們永遠知道這次估得有多準。
6. 邊界與收尾4:15–4:45 One thing we are careful about: this is measurement, not a carbon credit — and lettuce is the test crop, not the carbon sink. What LeafCarbon builds is a low-cost, non-destructive ruler. Once calibrated, the same approach extends to the carbon pools that actually matter — forests, soils, restoration sites — where cheap measurement is exactly what is missing today. 畫面：簡報 Slide 7（Scope）→ Slide 8（Close）。先打出「measurement ≠ credit · lettuce ≠ sink」誠實邊界，再切森林／土壤／復育場景，LeafCarbon 標誌定格。 有一件事我們很謹慎：這是量測，不是碳權；萵苣是測試作物，不是碳匯標的。LeafCarbon 做的是一把低成本、非破壞的尺。校正之後，同一套做法可以延伸到真正重要的碳庫——森林、土壤、生態復育——而那裡今天正缺的，就是便宜的量測。
